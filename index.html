Who We Are

Healing the soil, protecting cattle, and empowering rural communities.

Swavlambi Entrepreneur's Global Association (SEGA) is a registered, forward-thinking non-profit organisation dedicated to sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and community empowerment across Himachal Pradesh.

Founded in 2017, SEGA was born from a simple but powerful belief: the protection of cattle, the health of our soil, and the prosperity of rural communities are not separate goals. They are one.

Our work brings together the ancient wisdom of Gauvansh (cattle) care and the innovations of modern sustainable technology to address stray cattle welfare, chemical farming, rural unemployment, environmental degradation, and water scarcity.

"We are not just a Gaushala. We are a movement."
Our Purpose

Our Mission

We integrate traditional practices with modern technology to build a greener, self-sustaining future.

01

Enhance Gauvansh Welfare

Improve the overall health and well-being of Gauvansh through comprehensive medical care, proper shelter, and efficient water management.

02

Promote Environmental Sustainability

Utilize cow dung to produce eco-friendly products like bio plant pots and cow dung bricks, reducing waste and supporting sustainable practices.

03

Support Community Development

Create employment opportunities for local communities by involving them in fodder production, silage management, and biodegradable manufacturing.

04

Increase Green Cover

Partner with government and private agencies to plant more trees using bio plant pots, contributing to reforestation and urban green spaces.

05

Promote Organic / Natural Farming

Encourage organic farming by using cow dung as a natural fertiliser, reducing chemical dependency and enhancing soil health.

06

Promote Eco Homes (Gaucrete)

Promote construction of eco-friendly homes using Gaucrete (cow dung bricks) which are inexpensive and naturally reduce temperatures by 10-15°C.

Our Sanctuaries

On the Ground: Our Projects

Explore our active operations and rehabilitation centers across Himachal Pradesh.

Active Sanctuary

Handa Kundi Cattle Sanctuary

Operational since 10th October 2024

SEGA took over the Handa Kundi Cattle Sanctuary in Nalagarh, Baddi, on October 10, 2024. It is Himachal Pradesh's only exclusive bull sanctuary. Since taking over, we have reinforced the sheds with canvas tarpaulin and steel supports (funded by BBNDA), expanded the herd to approximately 500 Nandi bulls, and put 20 bighas under cultivation for Super Napier grass.

  • Land Area: 120 Bighas of sanctuary land.
  • Fodder: 20 Bighas dedicated to Super Napier cultivation.
  • Partner: BBNDA (Baddi Barotiwala Nalagarh Development Authority).

Active Rehabilitation Centre

Neripul Gau Sadan

Operational since 1st January 2025

Established on January 1, 2025, Neripul Gau Sadan is a dedicated rehabilitation center sheltering approximately 100 cows (expanding to 150). The facility ensures proper medical care, nutrition, and shelter, with a focus on intensive veterinary care for sick, old, or injured animals.

  • Focus: Medical rehabilitation and recovery.
  • Staff: Dedicated local caretakers and veterinary helpers.

Official Partner

Collaboration with BBNDA

SEGA is officially recognized and partnered with the Baddi Barotiwala Nalagarh Development Authority (BBNDA) for stray cattle management, gaushala operations, and sustainable infrastructure development.

BBNDA has entrusted SEGA with:

  • Stray Cattle Management: Relocating and rehabilitating stray cattle from the Municipal Solid Waste Plant at Kenduwal to SEGA’s sanctuaries.
  • Gaushala Operations: Providing shelter, nutritional food, and veterinary medical care.
  • Sustainable Infrastructure: Managing water storage systems, road connectivity, and afforestation efforts.
